🚵‍♂️ UCI MTB Calendar Sync

Never miss a mountain bike race again! Get all UCI Mountain Bike World Cup races, championships, and events automatically delivered to your phone or computer calendar.

🤔 What is this?

The UCI (Union Cycliste Internationale) is cycling's world governing body. They organize all the major mountain bike competitions - World Cups, World Championships, and continental events. Normally you'd have to manually check their website to see when races are happening.

This service automatically watches the UCI calendar and converts it into a format your calendar app can understand. Once you subscribe, you'll get automatic updates whenever new races are announced or schedules change.

🏁 What events will I see?

You'll get all official UCI Mountain Bike events including:

  • World Cup races - Downhill, Cross-Country, Short Track
  • World Championships - Annual world titles across all disciplines
  • Continental Championships - European, American, Asian, African, Oceanian titles
  • Major stage races - Cape Epic, Leadville, and other UCI-sanctioned events
  • Youth competitions - Junior and U23 championships

Events show up with race location, dates, and links to official information.

🤖 Automatic Updates: This calendar syncs with UCI weekly - you'll never have to manually update it!
